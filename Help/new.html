\n Select Privly Application\n

\n

\n This page summarizes the privacy of your Privly\n application bundle. We group applications according to the privacy they give\n your data. While applications may provide stronger privacy\n than is stated here, the motivating principle grouping apps is\n "users make mistakes." If you need to be an expert in an\n application to use it effectively, then it doesn\'t deserve \n a strong classification.\n
