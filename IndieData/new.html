Use this form to add your LinkedIn contact database. Steps:

  1. Export your data to csv by logging into LinkedIn and visiting their address book export tool
  2. Open the CSV file you downloaded and copy/paste it into the form below
  3. Click submit and hope that your format is correct...
  4. Now you can close this form and your data will be searchable by highlighting text in a form element on a website. The highlighted text will be used to search your contact database.
